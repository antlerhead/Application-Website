About

Hello World!!

My name is Max I am a creative person and I love making things. I am applying because I love coding. I’ve found that it allows me to use my creativity and abstract thinking to solve problems, which I find really fun and rewarding!

I can see myself working as a Developer and what I want for the future is to progress to a career in Tech. I have been learning independently and I have been looking for a programme that is the right fit for me.

By attending Hackathon’s and meet-ups I have found that I learn better through making and asking for advice when stuck. Working in a group or as part of a community would be the ideal as I believe that would allow me to build confidence and meet others who are learning as well. Also, the application process has given me the confidence to do things I had been too intimidated to try before. Such as going to tech meet-ups and to use Github.

I have used a lot of learning resources that people have setup to give back to the community, and I am waiting to have the opportunity to do the same. Being a Mentor sounds like the perfect way to do this. I hope when I am able, I can also introduce and help more people from different backgrounds to get into coding.
